Go to the Amazon website. To the right of the search box, go to “Account & Lists” and click on “Manage Your Content and Devices”.

Then go to the menu “Preferences” and click on “Personal Document Settings”.

Now you can add to your “Approved Personal Documents e-mail List”.

Now you have your personal e-mail address for your Kindle under “Send-to-Kindle e-mail Settings”. You can edit and change this address at any time.
